Levels
	In Main Menu
	Child the level Prefab into the level switcher object
	Now drag and drop the level object you just made into Level Select Switcher list
	Now change the image on the level prefab
	Finally go into the Play Button object of your level Prefab object
	Create a new on click event in your level prefab
	Drag the Scene Switcher Object into the play button on click event
	Select the function in the scene switcher called switchScenes
	Pass in your EXACT scene name as the parameter
	Press Play and test.
